# policies/exit_policy_v2.txt
Title: Exit Policy v2
Effective: 2024-04-01
Region: IN

1. Probation
Standard 3 months (may vary by role).

2. Notice Period
30 days during probation; 60 days after confirmation.

3. Resignation Process
Submit resignation via HRMS/Email; serve notice period unless waived.

4. Handover
Mandatory knowledge transfer; return company assets.

5. Final Settlement
Processed within 30 days of last working day; includes salary dues and statutory payouts.
