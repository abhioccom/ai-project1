# policies/leave_policy_v3.txt
Title: Leave Policy v3
Effective: 2025-01-01
Region: IN

1. Annual / Earned Leave (EL)
Full-time employees receive 12 days per year, credited monthly. Carry forward up to 30 days; encashment allowed only at exit.

2. Sick Leave (SL)
8 days per year. Medical certificate required if absent for >2 consecutive working days. Unused sick leave cannot be carried forward; it lapses at year-end.

2.3 Carry Forward
Sick leave does not carry forward and expires on Dec 31 each year.

3. Casual Leave (CL)
6 days per year for urgent/personal needs.

Special Leaves
Marriage: 8 days; Bereavement: 3 days (immediate family).
